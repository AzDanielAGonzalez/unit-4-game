! Crystal Game

Crystals Collector!

How to play:


  1. At the start of every game you will be given a random number.
  2. There are four crystals bellow.
    • Blue
    • Red
    • Green
    • Yellow
  3. Click on a crystal to find out it's hidden number value, that number will be added to your total score.
  4. You win the game when you match your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score is above the random number.
